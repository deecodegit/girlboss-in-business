## module 1: foundations (how culture actually works)

concepts

* culture as a system (not vibes)  
* base vs superstructure (marx, gramsci)  
* habitus (bourdieu)  
* symbolic capital

outcomes

* stop moralizing consumer behavior  
* understand why people buy “illogical” things

exercises

* map your own consumption as class signaling  
* rewrite one brand’s success as cultural alignment

## module 2: india as a multi-speed country

study

* urban vs peri-urban vs rural india  
* tier 1 / 2 / 3 beyond income  
* regional capitalism differences

cities to deep-dive

* mumbai: hustle \+ proximity anxiety  
* delhi: power, status, performance  
* bengaluru: merit \+ globalized self  
* kolkata: intellectual capital \+ nostalgia  
* chennai: discipline \+ inward pride  
* hyderabad: wealth without loudness  
* goa: leisure as identity (you know this one)

exercise

* same product, 7 cities: how would it be positioned differently?

## module 3: generational india

cohorts

study

framework

* when to provoke  
* when to comfort

final project
